# Video Question

"Why did you start working on this project?"

or

"What made you want to build Roofwander?"

---

## Potential Answers

### Seb
**Motivation:** product + freedom to build

I already owned a rooftop tent and loved the experience.

It felt natural to build something around that world.

I also liked the idea of working on a project where we could use different tools and not be stuck in a very structured environment.

Learning by building something real felt valuable, even before thinking about financial outcomes.

### Lucas
**Motivation:** entrepreneurship + road trips

I was studying entrepreneurship and initially needed a project for school.

I've always loved road trips and outdoor travel.

The idea quickly became more than just a school project and turned into something we wanted to build seriously.

### Sam
**Motivation:** technical challenge + marketplaces

I saw the potential of the idea early on.

What interested me most was the technical side.

Building a marketplace involves front-end, back-end, infrastructure, and now AI tools, which makes it a very exciting problem to work on.

---

## Concluding Question

"Different motivations, but why did you all decide to build it together?"

**Possible answer:**

Because we were aligned on the direction and excited about the opportunity to build something meaningful together.
