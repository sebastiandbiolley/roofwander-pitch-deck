How do you get known within a niche industry?

Not by users but by the players already in the ecosystem.

In the rooftop tent world that means:

• brands
• resellers
• rental companies
• outdoor communities
• event organizers

At the beginning, nobody really knows you.

So the question becomes:

How do you enter the conversation?

Some things we focus on:

Partnerships

Reaching out to brands, resellers, and communities connected to rooftop tents.

The goal is not only promotion, but understanding how everyone in the ecosystem works.

Events

Attending festivals and outdoor events where the industry gathers.

These moments make it much easier to meet people, exchange ideas, and explain what you are building.

Content

Publishing guides and articles around rooftop tents.

It helps show that you are genuinely involved in the space.

Still learning how these industries actually function.
