Creating SEO pages is easy.

Understanding if they are actually useful is harder.

While working on SEO for Roofwander, we realized ranking alone is not enough.

A page can get impressions and still provide little value.

So we monitor how users interact with content once they land on the site.

Some signals we look at:

• time per session
• bounce rate
• navigation paths between pages

These metrics help answer simple questions:

Are people actually reading the page?

Do they explore other parts of the marketplace?

Or do they leave immediately?

This helps us understand which pages provide useful information and which ones need improvement.

SEO is not only about getting traffic.

It's also about making sure the content actually helps users once they arrive.
