One thing we realized while building a marketplace:

Listings alone are not enough for SEO.

A listing page usually targets a very specific search.
But most users start their search much earlier.

They search things like:

• rooftop tent brands
• road trip ideas
• where to camp
• how rooftop tents work
• comparisons and guides

So we started building pages around the whole ecosystem, not just rentals.

For example:

• location pages
• brand pages
• guides
• blog articles

The idea is simple.

Capture different search intents, not only the final rental query.

Then connect everything through internal links so Google and users can navigate the ecosystem.

Listings become the core, and other pages help bring discovery traffic.
