One question we keep asking ourselves while building Roofwander.

How many languages are too many for SEO?

International SEO is powerful.

Translating pages allows us to reach users searching in different markets.

But it also creates a new challenge.

Each language generates many additional pages:

• listings
• guides
• blog articles
• location pages
• brand pages

Very quickly the number of indexed pages grows.

At some point we start wondering:

Does this help Google understand the site better?

Or can too many localized pages dilute signals and make SEO harder?

Still experimenting with this.

Curious how others approach multilingual SEO.

Do you translate everything or focus only on the most important pages?
