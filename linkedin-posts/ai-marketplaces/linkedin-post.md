How will AI change marketplaces compared to SaaS?

Something I've been thinking about while building Roofwander.

AI is already transforming many software products.

In SaaS, the impact is clear:

AI can automate tasks, generate content, analyze data, and sometimes replace entire tools.

But marketplaces might be different.

A marketplace is not only software.
It is a network of supply and demand.

So the question becomes:

What does AI actually disrupt in marketplaces?

Some possibilities:

• AI helping users discover the right product faster
• AI recommending listings based on preferences
• AI helping suppliers create better listings
• AI improving search and filtering
• AI assisting customer support

But the core value of a marketplace might remain the same.

Supply.
Demand.
Trust.

AI may change how people navigate marketplaces, but not necessarily why they exist.

Still early days, but it's an interesting space to watch.

Curious how others see this.

Do you think AI will disrupt marketplaces as much as SaaS?

Or will it mainly improve discovery and user experience?
