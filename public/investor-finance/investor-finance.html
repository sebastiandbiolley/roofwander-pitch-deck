Rental activity (core marketplace)

1. Demand (top-down)

We start from high-intent Google search volume for queries like "rooftop tent rental". This represents people actively looking to rent, not general interest.

2. Geographic rollout

We scale demand based on where we operate:

  • Y1: limited markets (BE, FR partial)
  • Y2: expansion (DE, NL, IT)
  • Y3: broader European coverage

→ Demand grows as new countries go live

3. Formula

Bookings = Demand × Capture rate

We apply a conservative conversion rate (search → booking) that improves with liquidity, trust, and acquisition efficiency.

Capture rate: Y1 2.4% · Y2 7.3% · Y3 12.1%

We convert real search demand into bookings, scaling with rollout and improving execution.

Monetization

How we estimate affiliate sales

1. Two demand sources

Affiliate sales come from:

  • Rental conversions (~10%) — renters who decide to buy after trying
  • Buy-side demand (market capture) — people directly searching to purchase

2. Market-based capture

We estimate total tents sold using public market data and apply a conservative capture rate that grows as SEO, comparison pages, and brand awareness improve. Buy-side becomes increasingly independent from rentals.

Capture rate: Y1 0.0% · Y2 0.56% · Y3 1.56%

3. Final logic

Affiliate sales = (10% × rental users) + (capture rate × total tents sold)

Affiliate combines rental conversion with independent market capture.
