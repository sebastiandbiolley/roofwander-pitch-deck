Content Marketing Intern

Marketing · Remote-friendly · Startup Brand content, ad creatives, and performance marketing with the founders. View description

About the role

Are you a student or recent graduate eager to learn how brand and marketing content is created, used, and measured in a real startup? Join Roofwander as a Content Marketing Intern, where you’ll work hands-on on brand content and ad creatives, and gain exposure to how this content performs in real marketing campaigns (Meta / Google Ads).

This role combines learning with real responsibility. You’ll work closely with the founders, contribute to live marketing initiatives, and see how content impacts brand visibility, user acquisition, and performance marketing results.

What you’ll do & learn

  • Create and manage brand and marketing content across digital channels
  • Produce content related to Roofwander and outdoor travel
  • Design and adapt ad creatives for Meta Ads and Google Ads
  • Work closely with the founders to learn how ad audiences are built and how performance marketing works
  • Understand how content performance is measured, analyzed, and improved
  • Maintain and evolve Roofwander’s visual and content identity
  • Execute and manage UGC content plans (collaborations with creators and partners)
  • Contribute to offline marketing assets (flyers, cards, stands, merch)

What we’re looking for

Education: Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Marketing, Communications, Design, or a related field.

Experience: Some experience with content creation, social media, or branding (school projects, personal projects, internships, or freelance work).

Languages: English (additional languages are a plus).

Skills & mindset

  • Strong interest in content creation and brand marketing
  • Ability to execute and manage content independently
  • Creative, proactive, and detail-oriented
  • Comfortable coordinating with external creators and partners

Nice to have

  • Experience with Canva, Figma, or similar tools
  • Interest in marketing, ads, or product
  • Interest in outdoor travel, camping, or road trips

Why Roofwander?

  • Hands-on role with real responsibilities, not just observation
  • Direct collaboration with the founders
  • Learn how content, ads, and brand work together in practice
  • Flexible, online working setup
  • Opportunity to travel and work together in different cities as the team meets in person
  • Opportunity to grow quickly in an early-stage startup environment
