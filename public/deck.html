Market Context

Rooftop tent travel is booming

Outdoor travel is accelerating. Rooftop tents are emerging as a flexible alternative to camper vans.

0× Search interest growth
Show chart

Source: Google Trends, 2016 – March 2026. Relative search interest (0–100).

Major outdoor brands entered over the last 5 years

A rooftop tent is a foldable sleeping system mounted on a vehicle's roof rack.

Typical characteristics:

  • Price range: €1,200 – €4,000+
  • Installed on vehicle roof bars
  • Setup time typically under 5 minutes
  • Allows sleeping anywhere while road-tripping

Two main categories:

Soft shell
  • lighter
  • lower price
  • manual unfolding
Hard shell
  • faster setup
  • more durable
  • higher price

AI makes it cheaper than ever to build marketplaces, but not to build liquidity.

The real opportunity is to move early, build supply and community, and create network effects before the category consolidates.

THE PROBLEM

Rooftop tents remain underutilized in a fragmented market

Rooftop tent demand is growing, but access, usage, and decision-making remain inefficient.

Idle assets
  • Tents are used only 10 to 30 nights per year
  • Owners hesitate to rent due to hassle and risk
  • No simple, trusted way to monetize idle tents
Limited rental access
  • Few rental operators
  • Availability tied to specific locations and dates
  • Limited tent models available for trial
Difficult purchase decisions
  • Buyers rely mostly on specs and marketing
  • Large price differences between models
  • Hard to compare tents based on actual use

This creates a gap between unused rooftop tents and travelers who want to try rooftop camping. A gap few players are solving.

THE SOLUTION

A trusted rental marketplace powering smarter buying

Roofwander combines rentals, real-world reviews, and product discovery in one system.

Rental-first marketplace
  • Owners and professional partners rent safely
  • Roofwander handles bookings, payments, deposits, and insurance

Comparison-first buy side

European launch strategy

Roofwander launches in Europe starting with markets where rooftop tent demand and supply already exist.

Initial focus markets:

  • Belgium
  • France

These markets combine:

  • growing rooftop tent adoption
  • strong road-trip culture
  • dense travel networks

Second expansion wave:

  • Germany

Germany represents the largest rooftop tent market in Europe and anchors the next wave across the rest of the continent.

The platform expands region by region, building supply density and local liquidity before entering new markets.

Beyond Europe, the U.S. rooftop tent market is estimated to be around 1.5× the size of Europe, creating a meaningful long-term expansion opportunity once the European model is validated.

UNIT ECONOMICS

Marketplace CAC is constrained by per-booking contribution margin

Both sides of the marketplace matter. We lay out our assumptions so you can stress-test them.

~€40
Margin per booking

€200 avg booking, 15% take rate, minus ops costs. Room to grow with insurance and affiliate.

2x / 20x
Bookings per user

A renter books ~2x. An owner hosts ~20x. One owner supports ~10 renters.

< €40
Max CAC per booking

Each booking must earn more than it costs to acquire. Current ceiling: €36/booking (renter), €4/booking (owner).

Renter acquisition

  • SEO + paid search
  • Referrals

Owner acquisition

  • Community + direct outreach
  • Referrals

CAC allocation shifts depending on the bottleneck: supply or demand.

  • More listings and reviews improve conversion and trust
  • SEO and content compound into organic traffic over time
  • Referrals and brand awareness grow with usage

As liquidity and organic discovery improve, acquisition becomes cheaper and more predictable.
