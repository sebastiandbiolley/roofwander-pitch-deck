Major outdoor brands have entered the category in the past 5 years

A rooftop tent is a foldable sleeping system mounted on a vehicle’s roof rack.

Typical characteristics:

  • Price range: €1,200 – €4,000+
  • Installed on vehicle roof bars
  • Setup time typically under 5 minutes
  • Allows sleeping anywhere while road-tripping

Two main categories exist:

Soft shell
  • lighter
  • lower price
  • manual unfolding
Hard shell
  • faster setup
  • more durable
  • higher price
