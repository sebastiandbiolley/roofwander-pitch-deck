Rental generates cheap traffic, trust, and real usage data that we monetize above just rental and it strengthens the platform over time.

Payouts are released after the trip, with disputes and support managed centrally.

Simple economics:

  • owners set prices (typically €20–€60 / night)
  • average booking value is around €200
  • Roofwander takes ~15% commission per booking
Pro rentals
  • lower CAC through B2C and B2B relationships
  • more availability and model coverage
  • strong inbound demand from outdoor shops and rental operators
Buy side
  • comparison-first product discovery based on real trips
  • ~10% affiliate commissions on rooftop tent sales
  • ~€220 average revenue per sale
Pro Hub
  • brands and resellers connect—quotes, product info, analytics
  • tools and pro features; after marketplace validation

Non-rental revenue can reach ~50% of total revenue by Year 3.
