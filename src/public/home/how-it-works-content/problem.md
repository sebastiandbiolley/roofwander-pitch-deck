# Problem

## Section 1 — The Problem

Rooftop tents have grown popular for road trips and outdoor travel—they turn almost any car into a mobile camping setup. Yet the market stays fragmented and inefficient: many tents sit unused while travelers struggle to access them.


<div class="doc-problem-cards">
  <div class="doc-problem-card">
    <div class="doc-problem-card-header">
      <span class="doc-problem-card-number">1</span>
      <h3 class="doc-problem-card-title">Idle Equipment</h3>
    </div>
    <p class="doc-problem-card-lead">Rooftop tents cost €1,200–4,000+ and sit unused most of the year.</p>
    <ul class="doc-problem-card-list">
      <li>10–30 nights of use per year</li>
      <li>Difficulty renting privately</li>
      <li>Concerns around damage, trust, logistics</li>
    </ul>
    <p class="doc-problem-card-outcome">Many tents remain unused for most of the year.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="doc-problem-card">
    <div class="doc-problem-card-header">
      <span class="doc-problem-card-number">2</span>
      <h3 class="doc-problem-card-title">Limited Rental Options</h3>
    </div>
    <p class="doc-problem-card-lead">For travelers, renting is difficult and options are scarce.</p>
    <ul class="doc-problem-card-list">
      <li>Professional operators can be expensive</li>
      <li>Availability tied to locations and dates</li>
      <li>Few tent models in rental fleets</li>
    </ul>
    <p class="doc-problem-card-outcome">Hard for travelers to try rooftop camping.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="doc-problem-card">
    <div class="doc-problem-card-header">
      <span class="doc-problem-card-number">3</span>
      <h3 class="doc-problem-card-title">Difficult Purchase Decisions</h3>
    </div>
    <p class="doc-problem-card-lead">Buying is confusing without hands-on experience.</p>
    <ul class="doc-problem-card-list">
      <li>Specs and marketing only</li>
      <li>Limited reviews</li>
      <li>Wide price variance, unclear differences</li>
    </ul>
    <p class="doc-problem-card-outcome">The easiest way to understand rooftop camping? Try it first.</p>
  </div>
</div>
