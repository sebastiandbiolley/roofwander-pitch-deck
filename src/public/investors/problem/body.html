THE PROBLEM

Rooftop tents remain underutilized in a fragmented market

Rooftop tent demand is growing, but access, usage, and decision-making remain inefficient.

Idle assets
  • Tents are used only 10 to 30 nights per year
  • Owners hesitate to rent due to hassle and risk
  • No simple, trusted way to monetize idle tents
Limited rental access
  • Few rental operators
  • Availability tied to specific locations and dates
  • Limited tent models available for trial
Difficult purchase decisions
  • Buyers rely mostly on specs and marketing
  • Large price differences between models
  • Hard to compare tents based on actual use

This creates a gap between unused rooftop tents and travelers who want to try rooftop camping. A gap few players are solving.
